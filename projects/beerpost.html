Beer Post - Keep Track of beers you had

What was the Project?

Kengo Yoshii, an avid beer drinker, wanted to take a memo whenever he drinked a great beer at a bar, pub, and his beer friends house. He came up an idea of creating a mobile app that allows beer lovers easily rate, record, and share what they drinked. It's a SNS for beer lovers. It also had a feature to find the near beer bars based on your location.

Description

  • github.com/kengoy/beerpost
  • 2015-2016

  • Dear Beer Lovers, If you are looking for a simple app to keep track of your favorite beers, BeerPost is the one for you. You can evaluate them and leave a little note about the characteristics of the beers and how you felt about them. This app also let you find a beer bar near you for another great beer adventure.Cheers!

Runalarm - This will wake you up

What was the Project?

Kengo Yoshii, a hard worker till midnight, needed a powerful alarm that forces him to wake up in the morning. He came up an idea of creating a mobile app that let him wake up by forcing him to run to stop the alarm. The app used activity recognition API on Android, and CMMotionActivity iOS to detect the user is running.

It also provided Android Wear App for watch devices.

Description

  • github.com/kengoy/runalarm_ios github.com/kengoy/runalarm_android
  • 2014-2015

  • Runalarm will wake you up no matter what, since it requires running to stop the alarm. Runalarm is recommended for people who

    • needs to wake up on time
    • wants to exercise soon after waking up
    • wants to make a fresh start of the day
    • wants to use early morning time for some activity

    With this special alarm clock, you will never miss an important plan any more. You will always be on time and feeling great because your morning exercise is already done!
